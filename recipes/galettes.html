Galettes

A marginally healthier take on the classic crepe.



Ingredients

  • 2% Milk
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Eggs
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt


Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, eggs, and milk, adding a pinch of salt.
  2. Add cooking oil to pan, then pour mix thinly into pan.
  3. Flip when appropriate.
  4. Add ham, cheese and additional egg to crepe surface.
  5. Cover pan with lid. Cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Remove galette from pan, turn corners over as seen in the photo, and enjoy.
