Galettes

A simple, yet...simple meal, fit for any morning.



Ingredients

  • 2% Milk
  • Frosted Mini Wheats (boxed)
  • Imagination


Steps

  1. Procure a bowl and spoon.
  2. Pour Frosted Mini Wheats from box into bowl until 70% full.
  3. Add milk until milk level reaches 80% of bowl's capacity.
  4. Enjoy!
