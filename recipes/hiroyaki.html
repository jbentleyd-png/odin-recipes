Hiroshima Okonomiyaki

The world's best iteration of savory pancake.



Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yakisoba
  • Sengiri-sliced Cabbage
  • Egg
  • Thinly-sliced Pork
  • Water
  • Okonomi Sauce
  • Japanese Mayo


Steps

  1. Mix water and flour, then pour onto griddle in a pancake shape.
  2. Nearby, cook eggs in a circular shape.
  3. Nearby, cook pork in a circular shape.
  4. Add cabbage to the top of the partially cooked eggs.
  5. Once pork is cooked, add to the top of the pancake, and flip.
  6. Nearby, cook yakisoba with some watter in a circular pattern. Place eggs and cabbage on top.
  7. When satisfied, flip the pancake stack and place the egg stack on top of it.
  8. Top the okonomiyaki with mayo and sauce, and dig in!
